About the Journal

The International Multidisciplinary Scientific Journal is a quarterly open-access electronic publication. It brings together peer-reviewed research across education, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, economics, information technology, and digital innovation.

Our mission is to provide a professional academic platform for authors and readers, with clean static pages, fast performance, and metadata that supports Google Scholar indexing and DOI discovery.

Journal identity

  • Quarterly publication schedule
  • Academic minimalist style
  • Static HTML/CSS/JS architecture
  • Responsive and mobile friendly

Scope

We publish original research articles, review papers, and case studies that contribute to multidisciplinary scholarship and digital transformation.

  • AI and machine learning
  • Cybersecurity and data protection
  • Education and digital learning
  • Economics and information systems
