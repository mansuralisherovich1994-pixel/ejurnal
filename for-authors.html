For Authors

Author Submission Guidelines

Submit your manuscript with clear article metadata, a structured abstract, and DOI-ready information. All submissions must comply with journal formatting and open access policies.

Article requirements

  • Title, authors, affiliations
  • Abstract and keywords
  • Publication date and DOI link
  • PDF file or PDF link for full text

Submission process

Please send your manuscript and PDF files to the editorial email address. We support DOI registration with Zenodo or OSF for published articles.

Each published article receives a dedicated HTML page with semantic metadata for indexing.
