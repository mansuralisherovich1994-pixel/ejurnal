Publication Ethics

Ethical Standards

The International Multidisciplinary Scientific Journal follows ethical guidelines for peer review, author contributions, and publication integrity.

Peer review and integrity

  • Double-blind peer review
  • Clear authorship attribution
  • Plagiarism checks and originality

Publication responsibilities

  • Accurate metadata and DOI citation
  • Open access distribution
  • Transparent corrections and retractions
